Automations Demonstrated

Four production-ready automations are built and explained in the demo. Each one solves a real business problem and can be adapted to your specific tools and workflow.

📧

Customer Email Auto-Responder

An AI reads incoming customer emails, classifies the intent (support, sales, billing, general), drafts a contextually appropriate response, and routes it to the right team — all in under 30 seconds. Handles 80% of inquiries without human intervention.

🎯

Lead Scoring with AI

Automatically enrich new leads from your CRM with company data, analyze fit against your ideal customer profile using AI, and assign a score from 1-100. High-scoring leads get instant Slack notifications so your sales team never misses a hot prospect.

🔄

Content Repurposing Pipeline

Turn one blog post into a LinkedIn post, three tweets, an email newsletter snippet, and a YouTube script outline — automatically. The AI adapts tone, length, and format for each platform while maintaining your brand voice and key messaging.

📄

Invoice Processing Automation

Incoming invoices are parsed by AI to extract vendor, amount, line items, and due date. Data flows into your accounting system, a Slack notification goes to finance, and overdue invoices trigger automatic follow-up emails — zero manual data entry.
