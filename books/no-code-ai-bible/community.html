Why Join?

The No-Code AI Builders community is the place where readers of The No-Code AI Bible connect, share what they've built, and help each other solve real automation challenges.

📤

Share Templates

Post your custom templates and download templates built by other community members. Every shared template includes documentation and setup instructions — so you can learn from how others solve problems and adapt their approaches to your own workflows.

🤝

Get Help

Stuck on a module configuration? Can't figure out why your webhook isn't firing? Post your question and get help from experienced builders who've solved the same problem. Most questions get a useful response within a few hours — often faster than vendor support.

🏆

Weekly Challenges

Every week, a new automation challenge is posted — build a specific workflow using any platform you want. Challenges range from beginner-friendly (email sorter) to advanced (multi-step AI pipelines). Winners get featured and earn community recognition.

📚

Template Library

Access a growing library of community-contributed templates organized by use case, platform, and complexity level. Every template is reviewed for quality before it's added to the library, so you know what you're downloading actually works in production.

Community Channels

Conversations are organized into dedicated channels so you can find exactly the help and inspiration you need without sifting through noise.

#no-code-beginners

New to no-code? This is your safe space. Ask any question without judgment — no question is too basic. Experienced members actively monitor this channel to help newcomers get unstuck and build confidence with their first automations.

#make-com

Deep-dive discussions about Make.com scenarios, modules, error handling, and optimization. Share your scenario screenshots, troubleshoot webhook issues, and learn advanced techniques like routers, iterators, and custom functions from power users.

#zapier

Everything Zapier: Zap configurations, multi-step workflows, Paths logic, Formatter tricks, and integration tips. Members share workarounds for Zapier's limitations and creative ways to push the platform beyond its obvious use cases.

#n8n

For the self-hosters and open-source enthusiasts. Discuss n8n deployment, custom nodes, Docker configurations, and scaling strategies. This channel is particularly active around new n8n releases, with members testing and reviewing new features together.

#template-sharing

The community's template exchange. Post your automation templates with a brief description of what they do and how to set them up. Browse templates from other members, remix them for your needs, and give feedback to help improve shared resources.

#wins

Celebrate your automation victories here. Saved your company 10 hours a week? Automated a process that used to take a full-time employee? Share the story, the stats, and the screenshots. Nothing motivates builders like seeing real results from real people.
