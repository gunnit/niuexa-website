How to Deploy These Prompts

Each template follows a proven three-step deployment process from Chapter 8.

Step 1

Customize the Variables

Each template has clearly marked placeholders for your business name, industry, services, pricing, and tone of voice. Fill these in once and the prompt adapts to your entire operation.

Step 2

Test with Real Scenarios

Run 10-20 test conversations covering normal cases, edge cases, and adversarial inputs. The guide includes a testing checklist and common failure modes to watch for.

Step 3

Deploy and Iterate

Start with low-stakes tasks and gradually expand scope. Monitor outputs weekly, track failure patterns, and refine prompts based on real usage data. The guide includes a prompt versioning system.
