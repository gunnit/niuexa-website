What the Demo Covers

Four critical capabilities that let a single operator handle the sales volume of a five-person team.

🎯

Automated Lead Qualification

The AI agent scores inbound leads in real time using custom criteria — budget, timeline, decision authority, and fit. Bad leads get a polite redirect. Hot leads get instant attention. You never waste time on tire-kickers again.

✉️

Personalized Follow-up Sequences

Every lead gets a tailored email sequence based on their specific pain points, industry, and engagement signals. The AI writes, schedules, and sends follow-ups that feel human — because each one is generated uniquely, not templated.

📅

Calendar Integration & Booking

When a lead is ready, the AI agent checks your real availability, proposes optimal meeting times, and handles the booking — including timezone conversion, reminder emails, and pre-meeting context summaries for you.

📊

CRM Data Sync

Every interaction, score change, and status update is automatically logged to your CRM. Deal stages advance based on actual engagement signals. You get a clean pipeline view without ever touching a data entry screen.

The Solo Sales Pipeline

Chapter 3 breaks down each stage of the pipeline so you can build your own version — no coding experience required.

Stage 1

Capture & Score

Leads arrive through your website, social media, or referral links. The AI agent instantly enriches each lead with public data, scores them against your ideal client profile, and routes them to the right sequence.

Stage 2

Nurture & Qualify

Automated email and messaging sequences engage each lead with relevant case studies, ROI projections, and social proof. The agent monitors open rates, replies, and click-through signals to gauge buying intent.

Stage 3

Book & Close

When engagement signals cross the threshold, the agent proposes a discovery call with your actual availability. Post-meeting, it sends a custom proposal draft for your review and handles follow-up until the deal closes.
