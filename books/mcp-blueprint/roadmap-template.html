Three Phases to Production

The roadmap is organized into three major phases, each with clear deliverables, decision gates, and rollback criteria.

🔍

Phase 1: Foundation

Weeks 1-4. Discover where MCP adds the most value in your organization. Audit existing AI tools, map sensitive data flows, and deploy a pilot server connected to a low-risk workflow. By the end of this phase, you have a working proof-of-concept and a validated architecture pattern.

🛠

Phase 2: Pilot

Weeks 5-8. Harden your pilot deployment for real workloads. Implement the security checklist from Chapter 3, set up governance policies, configure audit logging, and document compliance requirements. This phase transforms your proof-of-concept into an enterprise-grade integration.

🚀

Phase 3: Scale

Weeks 9-12. Train your team, onboard champion users, and roll out to production. Establish performance baselines, set up alerting and monitoring, and create the continuous improvement loops that keep your MCP deployment evolving as the protocol and your needs grow.
