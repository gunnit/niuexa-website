Community Guidelines

A few principles that keep this community valuable for everyone.

Ship, Don't Just Talk

We value action over theory. Share what you have built, what you are building, and what you learned from failures. Implementation stories beat abstract debates every time.

Help Generously

If you have been through it, help someone who is going through it now. Answer questions, share resources, and pay forward the help you received when you were starting out.

Stay Curious

AI moves fast. What worked last month may not work today. Approach every conversation with genuine curiosity and openness to new approaches, tools, and perspectives.

No Spam, No Pitches

This is a space for learning and building, not selling. Share your products in the designated channel, but keep discussions focused on helping each other succeed.
