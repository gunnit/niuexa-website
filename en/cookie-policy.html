Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 25, 2024

1. What are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us improve your browsing experience and provide personalized services.

2. Types of Cookies Used

Technical Cookies (Necessary)

These cookies are essential for website functionality:

  • Session cookies: to maintain an active session
  • Security cookies: to protect against cyber attacks
  • Preference cookies: to remember your settings

Legal basis: Legitimate interest (Art. 6, para. 1, lett. f GDPR)

Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site:

  • _ga: Distinguishes users (expiration: 2 years)
  • _ga_*: Maintains session state (expiration: 2 years)
  • _gid: Distinguishes users (expiration: 24 hours)

Legal basis: Consent (Art. 6, para. 1, lett. a GDPR)

Marketing Cookies (If applicable)

Third-party cookies may be used for:

  • Targeted advertising
  • Retargeting
  • Social media integration

Legal basis: Consent (Art. 6, para. 1, lett. a GDPR)

3. Third-Party Cookies

Our site may use third-party cookies:

  • Google Analytics: For anonymous statistical analysis
  • LinkedIn: For social sharing buttons
  • YouTube: For embedded videos (if present)

4. Cookie Management

You can manage your cookies in several ways:

Through Browser

All browsers allow you to:

  • View installed cookies
  • Block all cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Delete existing cookies

Through Cookie Banner

Upon first access to the site, a banner will appear to manage your cookie preferences.

5. Consent and Withdrawal

For non-technical cookies, we collect your explicit consent. You can:

  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Modify your preferences
  • Delete cookies from your browser

6. Consequences of Refusal

Refusing cookies may result in:

  • Limitations in browsing experience
  • Inability to use some features
  • Loss of personalized preferences

This Cookie Policy may be updated. We invite you to check it periodically for any changes.
